Rammstein

Rammstein is a German industrial metal band formed in 1994 by Till Lindmann (Lead singer). Other band members include Richard Kruspe (Lead guitar, backup singer), Christian Lorenz (Keyboard), Christoph Schneider (Drums, percussion), Paul Landers (Rythm guitar, backup vocals) , and Oliver Riedel (Bass guitar, backup vocals).

Notability

Rammstein is known for using pyrotechnics on stage. In their last performance, lead singer Till Lindmann, attached two flamethrowers to his forearms and shot flames over the crowd. One of their most famous uses of flames was when Lindmann attached ten foot wings to his back and shot flames out of them as well. They're liked so much because they like to "push" the boundaries and not care what people think.
